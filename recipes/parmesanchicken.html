The delicious chicken with cheese

This improvised recipe will take you to another dimension by cheesily melting in your mouth!

This recipe is really quick and easy to follow!

The cook time is 25 minutes, and the prep is only 10, so you'll be diving in really soon!

The ingredients list:

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
