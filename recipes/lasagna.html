The cheesiest Lasagna

This beautiful recipe allows for the most delicious lasagna you will ever taste!

Try it yourself!It's easy to make!

This recipe is cheap, quick, and easy to follow.Make sure you have all the essentials at your disposal before we get started.

In total, including the prep and the cook time, it will take you about an hour to taste this cheesy dish.

Here are the ingredients list:

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredder cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of riccota cheese
